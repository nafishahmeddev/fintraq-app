What's New in this Update (1.1.5)

App Lock Security
- Protect your financial data by enabling App Lock.
- Support for biometric authentication (Face ID / Fingerprint) or a secure 6-digit PIN.
- Automatically locks after a short grace period in the background to keep your data private.
- Configurable directly from Settings > Security.
